About Us

Welcome to Agre-biom Institute, a trailblazer in agricultural innovation since its inception in 1975, rooted in the vibrant fields of Karnataka, India. Over the past 49 years, we've evolved from pioneering bio-fertilizers to becoming a one-stop hub for cutting-edge agricultural solutions.

Today, Agre-biom Institute proudly offers a comprehensive range, including Harvestors, tractors, cold storage equipment, and all essentials for top-tier crop production. Embrace excellence with us as we continue to cultivate success in every harvest.
